My Favorite Classes & Teachers

The best part of the school day is hanging out in the high-tech science lab with Mrs. Ranjana, who accidentally dyed her hair purple during a potion-mixing experiment. We get to use glowing hologram projectors to study ancient dragon migration flights and map out deep space constellations. Even regular history class is wild because we get to wear virtual reality headsets and take field trips back in time to watch medieval castle battles.

The School Cafeteria & Recess Chaos

Lunchtime at our academy is a total blast thanks to the automated pizza conveyor belt and the legendary DIY ice cream sundae bar. Once we finish eating, everyone rushes out to the playground to climb the giant three-story treehouse slide or challenge the teachers to a match in the virtual reality sports arena. It is the perfect place to burn off energy before we head back inside for afternoon gaming and robot building.
